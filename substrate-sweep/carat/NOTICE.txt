CARAT catalogue subset: provenance
==================================

This directory contains the dimension-4 Q/Z-class catalogue tables of
CARAT (Crystallographic AlgoRithms And Tables), redistributed unmodified:

  Source:    https://github.com/lbfm-rwth/carat
  Commit:    65619d6ae4ec911c8cf7aa3c9fa09866a0d36ab9 (2024-12-20)
  Retrieved: 2026-06-04
  Subset:    tables/qcatalog/dim4/ (910 files, ~0.84 MB), one file per
             Z-class representative of the 227 conjugacy classes of
             finite subgroups of GL(4,Z) (Brown-Bulow-Neubuser-
             Wondratschek-Zassenhaus 1978, as distributed in CARAT).

Byte-level integrity was verified against the upstream repository at the
commit above (git working tree clean; no file modified).

groups and their subgroups for the substrate-uniqueness result.
